Aashiqui 2 is a 2013 Indian Hindi-language romantic musical drama film directed by Mohit Suri. The story follows Rahul Jaykar (Aditya Roy Kapur), a successful singer struggling with alcoholism, whose career is waning due to his addiction. One night, he encounters Aarohi Keshav Shirke (Shraddha Kapoor), a talented singer performing in a bar. Impressed by her voice and simplicity, Rahul promises to transform Aarohi into a singing sensation. As Aarohi’s career ascends, the two fall deeply in love. However, Rahul’s persistent addiction threatens their relationship and Aarohi’s burgeoning success, leading to emotional turmoil and heart-wrenching decisions.

Actors in Aashiqui 2

  • Aditya Roy Kapur as Rahul Jaykar – A famous singer battling alcoholism.
  • Shraddha Kapoor as Aarohi Keshav Shirke – A talented aspiring singer.
  • Shaad Randhawa as Vivek – Rahul's best friend and manager.
  • Mahesh Thakur as Saigal – Rahul’s mentor.
